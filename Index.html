Our Process

1. Patient Registration

Collecting patient demographics and insurance information accurately.

2. Insurance Verification

Verifying insurance coverage to ensure services are covered.

3. Charge Capture

Recording services provided to the patient for billing.

4. Claim Submission

Submitting claims to insurance companies in a timely manner.

5. Payment Posting

Posting payments received from insurers and patients accurately.

6. Denial Management

Addressing and correcting denied claims to recover lost revenue.

7. Patient Billing

Sending bills to patients and following up on unpaid balances.

8. Reporting

Providing detailed reports to track performance and identify areas for improvement.
